Architecture

How Lowkie Works

Five privacy layers working together.

01

Denomination Routing

Your deposit is decomposed into fixed 1.0, 0.1, and 0.01 SOL notes, then routed into matching pool tiers to reduce amount correlation.

Client-Side
02

Recipient Encryption

Recipient stored as SHA256(secret ∥ pubkey). The actual recipient is invisible on-chain until withdrawal.

On-Chain
03

MPC Encryption

Arcium's multi-party computation encrypts pool balances and note amounts. No single node ever sees plaintext.

Arcium MXE
04

Relayer Unlinkability

Withdrawal uses a different keypair than deposit with randomised timing, breaking the on-chain link.

Protocol
05

Silent Withdrawal

Direct lamport manipulation from program-owned PDA. Zero inner instruction logs. Block explorers show nothing.
